THE STORY OF LMI

LETS CONNECT

Established in 1998, Laser Modeling Israel has over 25 years of experience of helping companies from a wide range of industries to produce exceptional products during any and every phase of the production process. From the ideation and design to the manufacturing and post-processing methods. In Laser Modeling we have created the engineering solutions’ one-stop-shop for any technological challenge a manufacturer would encounter, even in the most complicated R&D processes.

Our excellence derives from the use of cutting-edge equipment and software, the strictest quality assurance standards and a full privacy guarantee. Through our adaptive, yet uncompromising practice, we deliver the best product quality, created using the highest precision techniques, while still providing quick response intervals, and unmatched customer service.

Our dedicated team members are highly skilled technical specialists possessing extensive experience in a variety of industries that match the diversity of our clients. Every day we meet new clients and new challenges, keeping us sharp minded, creative, and innovative when we require to provide the best service for our clients.

Our clientele spans a wide range of industries, from high-tech, biomedical, and national security organizations through start-ups and prime industrial manufactures. We have a portfolio of over 1600 active clients, among them are the most influential companies in Israel, that choose to put their trust on LMI’s professional services on their most important R&D processes.

LMI is dedicated to the highest Quality Assurance provided by the delivery of superb parts and models, while guaranteeing absolute data security. We are highly experienced with working under the strictest customers’ requirements of the most complicated and classified projects in Israel.

Our professional and uncompromising working practice had awarded us with an ISO 9001:2015 quality certificate, given by the IQC. Our working standards are examined and inspected by IQC on a yearly basis, guaranteeing that we are kept in the frontline of the Quality Assurance practices.

Laser Modeling Israel refuses to compromise its quality for speed. Every individual part is put through rigorous quality inspections during the entire process, from concept to creation, so we could guarantee that our customers receive the highest-quality parts with their order specifications.

Working with Laser Modeling Israel, means that you and your company will get the full assurance that your quality and privacy are kept to the highest standards.

From the day of its incorporation, Laser Modeling’s core value had always been to provide exquisite service, using the global forefront of the manufacturing technology. This vision of ours always challenges us to be better, to be more professional and to constantly research for the next technological destination of our domain. This 25 years long journey would not have been the same without the partners we have acquired along the way. Laser Modeling Israel is proud to be a part of a long-standing work relationship with the leading manufacturers in the 3D printing business.

01

Among our partners is Stratasys, a global leader in 3D printing and additive solutions, materials, and services, allowing us to deliver speed, innovation, performance, and full customization to our clients. Stratasys printers had been with us since our first days, and harnessing their technology granted us the knowledge and expertise to lead in the FDM and Polyjet domains.

02

Another key partner is HP, one of the world leaders in additive manufacturing printers thanks to the groundbreaking MJF 3D printing technology. The partnership with HP had allowed Laser Modeling to cross a paramount threshold, separating between the world of Rapid Prototyping, and the world of Rapid Production. The latter is a game changer in the service bureaus domain, making the manufacturing of small to medium sized 3D printed production series possible.

03

Lastly, joins our newest partner- AMT, a 3D Printing technology company dedicated to unlocking 3D printing as a viable alternative to traditional manufacturing through its suite of post-production hardware. PostPro, developed by AMT, is a digital post-production technology platform that automates the manual and costly steps associated with legacy additive manufacturing post processing. These abilities allow LMI to enable functional ‘high-volume end-use parts’ production from all of our 3D printers.

It is a privilege to work in such an innovative domain and to prosper together with its technological leaders.
